FAQ


What is Paer?

Paer is an iOS application that allows users in real-world proximity to instantly share contact information and connect on multiple social platforms instantly. Smarter than business cards and more convenient than manually punching in new contacts


Who are we?

We are five Seattle high school seniors and a college freshman who set out to design a mobile application that can turn strangers into friends, colleagues and connections.


How did we start?

Paer started in January of 2015 at a San Francisco hackathon under the name Linkt. We built a proof of concept around our desire to make social media truly social and this caught the attention of peers and even a few tech companies. Linkt evolved into Knockit and Knockit became Paer.
